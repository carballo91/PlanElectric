How We Work

A simple, predictable process—so you know what’s happening and why.

1) Consult & Scope

Share your goals and any photos. We discuss options, code needs, and ballpark pricing. For bigger jobs, we visit to finalize scope.

2) Proposal & Scheduling

Receive a clear proposal with scope, pricing, and timeline. We coordinate around your schedule and handle permits where required.

4) Final Check & Wrap-Up

We test everything, walk you through results, and provide any documentation needed for inspections or future service.
